Project

I Worked In AIR QUALITY MONITORING SYSTEM Project It is an minor project

Project Overview

The Air Quality Monitoring System aims to provide real-time data on air pollution levels by measuring various air quality parameters such as CO2, CO, NO2, PM2.5, PM10, temperature, and humidity. The system uses sensors to collect data, which is then processed and displayed on a web dashboard for easy monitoring and analysis.

Objectives

1.Continuous Monitoring: To continuously monitor air quality parameters in real-time. 2.Data Collection: To collect and store air quality data for historical analysis. 3.Alert Mechanism: To trigger alerts when pollution levels exceed predefined thresholds. 4.Public Awareness: To increase public awareness about air quality and its impact on health.
